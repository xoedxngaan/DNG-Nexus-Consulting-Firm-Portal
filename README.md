# Wix-Client-Facing-Information-Portal
Developed a simulated consulting firm website using Wix, designed as a client-facing information portal. The project emphasises structured information architecture and user flow design aligned with business requirements. See link to my site: https://zoedingaan.wixsite.com/consulting-firm

This project is a fully designed and developed simulation of a corporate website for a strategic consulting firm, "DNG Nexus." Built using Wix, it functions as a comprehensive client-facing information portal. The primary goal was to architect a professional digital presence that effectively communicates a complex service offering, builds trust, and guides potential clients through a logical journey of discovery.

# Core Objectives & Execution:
This project was an exercise in structured information architecture and user-centric design, translating business requirements into a functional digital experience.

# Strategic Information Architecture: The site's structure was designed to lead a visitor logically:
  1. Establish Credibility: The "Our Story" section immediately grounds the firm in a core philosophy ("clarity as a competitive advantage").
     
  2. Define Value Proposition: The "Positioning with Precision" banner and "Commitment to Excellence" section clearly state the services and methodology.
   
  3. Demonstrate Expertise: The "Services" and "Meet the Team" pages build confidence in the firm's capabilities.
     
  4. Prove Outcomes: The "Success Stories" section (with specific, data-driven case studies in Retail, Finance, and Healthcare) provides critical social proof and demonstrates tangible value.
    
  5. Establish Social Proof: The "Some of Our Clients" section further builds trust and authority.
      
· User Flow Design: The navigation and content hierarchy are intentionally designed to mirror a client's natural journey—from initial curiosity ("Who are they?") to validation ("Can they deliver?") and finally to action ("How do I engage?"). The clean, professional layout minimizes friction and reinforces the brand's message of "precision."

· Brand & Content Consistency: All copy was developed to maintain a consistent, professional tone aligned with a high-end consulting brand. The language is confident, precise, and focused on client outcomes—mirroring the firm's value proposition.

# Skills Demonstrated:
This project showcases skills directly applicable to roles in risk, compliance, and business analysis:

· Requirements Analysis: Translating the abstract concept of a "consulting firm" into a concrete set of content and structural requirements.

· Process & Workflow Design: Mapping the user's informational journey to ensure key messages are delivered at the right stage.

· Attention to Detail: Ensuring consistency in branding, tone, and visual presentation across all pages.

· Risk Mitigation (by design): A clear, professional, and informative site mitigates the risk of client misunderstanding or miscommunication before formal engagement begins.

· Communication: The entire site is an exercise in communicating complex, intangible services clearly and persuasively.
